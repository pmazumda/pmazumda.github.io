                    Disable Session Replication in Jboss EAP



Remove the <distributable/> tag from the application's WEB-INF/web.xml 

The above  steps applies to the following JBoss EAP versions:

  • 4.x
  • 5.x
  • 6.x
  • 7.x

                Disabling session replication from application, even though Jboss setup is clustered and load balancing is enabled  is a valid configuration and entirely depends upon the application requirement.
